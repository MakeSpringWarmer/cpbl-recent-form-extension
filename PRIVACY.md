# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-16

This extension does not collect, sell, transmit, or share personal data.

The extension runs only on supported CPBL official pages and reads public baseball statistics from CPBL official pages. Public statistics are cached locally for up to six hours.

The selected recent-game counts, custom date ranges, and player comparison mode are stored with Chrome Sync storage. If Chrome Sync is enabled, Chrome may sync those preferences through the user's Google account. The developer cannot access this synced preference data.

No analytics service, remote backend, account system, advertising system, or third-party tracking is used. Removing the extension deletes its locally stored data; synced preferences can also be managed through the user's Chrome Sync settings.

For privacy questions, contact the publisher through the project's GitHub repository: https://github.com/MakeSpringWarmer/cpbl-recent-form-extension
